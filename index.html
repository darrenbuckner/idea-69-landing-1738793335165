Executive Overview

A platform that simplifies the creation, chaining, and saving of generative AI workflows by allowing users to easily craft prompts and seamlessly route them through APIs to different AI services, such as ChatGPT and Leonardo AI.

Problem Statement

Businesses and creative professionals struggle with manually integrating multiple generative AI tools, which hinders efficient workflow creation and the ability to capture and reuse complex AI outputs.

Solution Statement

PromptFlow provides a simple, intuitive interface to create prompts, automatically routes them through various generative AI services via API integrations (e.g., from ChatGPT to Leonardo AI), and saves the entire workflow along with the outputs for future reuse, streamlining creative and operational processes.
